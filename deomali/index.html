About Us

St. Xavier School, Rajnagar, Odisha is a reputed educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds with a perfect blend of academic excellence and moral values. Established with the vision of providing quality education in a disciplined and inspiring environment, the school focuses on the holistic development of students—intellectually, socially, and emotionally. With well-qualified faculty, modern infrastructure, and a student-centric approach, St. Xavier School encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. Alongside academics, the school emphasizes co-curricular activities, character building, and leadership skills, ensuring that students grow into responsible and confident individuals ready to face future challenges.
